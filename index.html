What is Aprilvis?

City Soundscapes Analysis

Appraisal of Recordings at Interesting Locations: the Visualisation of Soundscapes.

Have you always wondered what the sonic environment of your city is? Our product goes beyond measuring decibels, it measures quality of sounds.

In just three simple steps. 1) uploading a soundfile of at least 20 minutes. 2) let the system do the magic. 3) interpret the chart and use the data.

This gives you more detailed and useful information than just decibels. The sound quality is visualized in an easy chart and also shows which sound sources are present at the location. For example, do you live in a quiet neighborhood with a park around the corner, or do you live downtown with a lot of cars.

Our product goes beyond measuring decibels, it measures the sound quality. We don't just measure sound levels (db) we measure sound quality.

We made the sound quality visible in an easy chart.

Don’t just measure decibels. Measure how we like our sonic environments! Do we find it lively, calm, boring or chaotic? This product knows.
